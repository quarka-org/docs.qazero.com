# Behavior

The **Behavior** report shows how users interact with your website.  
You can see which pages they visit and how they navigate through the site.

QA ZERO excludes visits shorter than one second.  
This helps focus on users who actually engaged with the page.

---

## Analysis views

You can analyze pages from the following three perspectives:

---

### Landing Pages

Shows the **first page** users visited on your site.

Use this to understand which pages attract traffic from search or ads.

---

### Trending Landing Pages

Shows landing pages with **increasing traffic**.

This is calculated by comparing the first 7 days and the last 7 days within the selected period.

Use this to identify trends or measure the impact of newly published content.

---

### All Pages

Shows statistics for **all pages** viewed on the site.

Use this to find:

- Popular content  
- Pages with high exit rates  

---

## Tips for analysis

Combine this report with other features for deeper insights:

- If a page has a high exit rate, check the **Heatmap** or **Session Recording** to understand user behavior  
- Use **Goals** to filter data and identify landing pages that lead to conversions
